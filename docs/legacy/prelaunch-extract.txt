RescueNet360 Website – Final Pre-Launch Review
This document consolidates persona-based feedback and a structured QA checklist to validate launch
readiness for rescuenet360.com.
Persona-Based Feedback
1) Pet Families (Lost Pet – High Emotion / High Urgency)
What they are thinking:
"My pet is missing. Tell me what to do in the next 5 minutes."
What the site must do:
Provide a clear emergency-focused CTA above the fold
Reduce cognitive load
Provide reassurance about privacy and safety
Clearly explain what happens after uploading a pet
Potential Friction:
Too much ecosystem language before practical steps
"Request Info" feeling like a sales funnel instead of help
Recommended Enhancements:
Add a "Lost pet right now?" section on Home + Families page:
Upload photo / Create Pet Identity
Mark as Lost
Network notified
Verified reunification
Add immediate value guidance even pre-launch
2) Shelter / Rescue Leadership
What they are thinking:
"Will this increase workload? Does it integrate? Is it secure?"
What the site must do:
Lead with operational outcomes:
Fewer calls
Faster owner matches
Reduced unnecessary intake
